Create the account in the app

  1. Launch Lakeview Lister and wait for the access screen to appear.
  2. Enter your email address and a password in the login form.
  3. Click Create Account.
  4. If your environment requires email confirmation, confirm the email first, then come back and sign in.
  5. Click Sign In once the account is ready.

Understand access state

Explore mode

You may be able to review setup areas before paying, but paid features stay locked until the account has active access.

Ready state

When access is active, the app is ready for draft building, publish actions, and live eBay listing workflows.

Choose a plan when you are ready

  1. Open Profile inside the app.
  2. Go to the Account area if you are not already there.
  3. Select the monthly or yearly plan shown in the UI.
  4. Complete checkout in the hosted billing flow.
  5. Return to the app and click Refresh Access.
After checkout

If access does not unlock immediately, return to the app and use Refresh Access so Lakeview Lister can pull the latest account state.
