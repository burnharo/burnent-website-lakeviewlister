How cancellation works for Pro subscriptions and future renewals.

Cancellation Policy (Summary)

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals but does not remove access you already paid for during the current billing period.

×

Cancel anytime

Use the billing portal or contact support to cancel. Your plan remains active until the end of the current paid billing period.

↻

No automatic downgrade surprise

After cancellation, paid tools stop renewing. Free listing sync remains available where supported by your app version and account status.

▣

eBay connection

Canceling Lakeview Lister does not automatically revoke eBay authorization or remove credentials from your app. You can revoke eBay access through your eBay account or developer settings.
