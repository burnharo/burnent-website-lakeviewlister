Subscription billing

Lakeview Lister is a subscription-based app. Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled. Subscription fees are non-refundable after a billing period begins, except where required by law or in cases of billing error. Your Lakeview Lister subscription does not include AI provider charges. Lakeview Lister requires you to add your own AI provider key, and model usage is billed separately by that provider under its own pricing and terms. Contact support if you need billing portal access to manage your subscription, update payment details, or view invoices.
