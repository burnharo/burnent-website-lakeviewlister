Refund Policy (Summary)

We do not offer refunds for subscription fees after a billing period begins, except where required by law or in cases of billing error.

No refunds after billing starts

Subscription fees are non-refundable once a billing period begins. Canceling stops future renewals, and your Pro access continues through the end of the current paid billing period.

If a free trial is offered, you can cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged. Once a paid billing period begins, the no-refunds policy applies.
